SRM is among India’s earliest large multidisciplinary higher educational institutions that now caters 50000+ students across 6 campuses. SRM’s Directorate of Admissions manages admissions for students from across India as well as for International students from all around the world.


SRM is often seen as a Mini-India. SRM is an ideal destination for those aspiring to pursue under-graduate, post-graduate, PhD or advanced studies. The promise of an enriching student life experience in a vastly multi-cultural, green and high ranked institution are but few reasons you must consider SRM for your higher education.

This will serve you as a gateway to understanding about the various programs offered, eligibility criteria, fees, scholarships and loan assistance. High Quality World Class Exposure At SRM

Back in 2003, SRM set up an International Advisory Board (IAB) consisting of 31 Distinguished Deans, Directors and professors from top universities across the world. SRM’s IAB is the first across the Indian University system. IAB works towards helping SRMIST set Global Standards and Internationalize through bringing globally acclaimed curriculum, research and teaching methodology to its students and faculty members. Now over 50+ Members are on IAB and they are from the best of the universities across the world including Harvard Business School, Kennedy Public School (Harvard University), MIT, Stanford, LSE, UC Berkeley, Cambridge, NUS among others.
